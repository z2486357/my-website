Snake Game


This is a game that you are a snake and you have to eat as many apple as you can.

The initial of the game. You are the green one and the apple is the red one. Slide on the screen to change the direction.


After you eat the apple, you will get one block longer and faster.
The apple will appear in another place.

If you crash on the wall or your body, you lose.
